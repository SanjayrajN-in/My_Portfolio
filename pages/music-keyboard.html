How to Use

Play

Use your computer keyboard or click the on-screen keys to play notes. Try different sound themes!

Record

Click Record, play some notes, then Stop when finished. Name your track and save it.

Layer

Create multiple tracks with different instruments, then play them all together for a rich sound.

Customize

Change which keys trigger which notes using the key mapping editor. Save your preferences.
